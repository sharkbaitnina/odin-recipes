Pho

Authentic, Vietnamese pho soup is all about the broth!

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 4 Pounds Beef Soup Bones
  • 1 (8 Ounce)Rice Noodles
  • 1 1/2 pounds Beef Top Sirloin
  • 2 1/2 Tablespoon of Fish Sauce
  • 1 Medium Onion, Unpeeled and cut in half
  • 5 Slices of Ginger
  • 2 pods Star Anise
  • 1 Tablespoon of Salt
  • Garnishes (Cilantro, Green Onion, Thai Basil, Lime, Hoisin Sauce, and Sriracha.)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  3. Place beef bones on a baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven until brown
  4. Place onion halves on a second baking sheet and roast on another rack until blackened and soft
  5. Transfer beef bones and onion halves to a large stockpot
  6. Add ginger, salt, star anise, fish sauce, and 4 quarts water, Bring to boil
  7. Reduce heat and simmer for 6 to 10 hours, Strain broth into a sauce pan
  8. Place rice noodles in a large bowl filled with room temperature water, soak for 1 hour
  9. Heat up some reserved broth and add rice noodles to cook
  10. Divide noodles among selves, top with Sirloin, cilantro and green oinion
  11. Ladle hot broth over the top, Stir and let sit until beef is partially cooked.
  12. Serve with bean sprouts, Thai basil, lime wedges, hoisin sauce, and chile-garlic sauce on the side.
